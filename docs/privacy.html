👋 Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled when you use the Pomodoro app. We value your privacy and are fully committed to protecting it through compliance with this policy.

By using the app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We keep it simple because we collect almost nothing.

🔒 Data Collection

Offline First: Pomodoro is designed to work completely offline. All your data — including focus sessions, streak information, and settings — are stored locally on your device in a private database.

No Tracking: We do not use any tracking pixels, analytics SDKs, or third-party cookies. We do not collect your name, location, or usage patterns for advertising purposes. Ever.

⚙️ How We Use Data

The data stored locally is used solely to provide the core functionality of the app, such as calculating your focus streaks and displaying your milestone progress.

This data never leaves your device. We have no servers receiving your personal information.

📋 Permissions

The app may request the following device permissions:

  • 🔔
    Notifications
    To alert you when a focus or break session ends so you never miss a transition.
  • 📳
    Vibration
    To provide tactile feedback when a timer completes, even when your phone is on silent.
