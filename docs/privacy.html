Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 09, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled when you use the Pomodoro app. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

Data Collection

Offline First: Pomodoro is designed to work offline. All your data, including focus sessions, streak information, and settings, are stored locally on your device in a private database.

No Tracking: We do not use any tracking pixels, analytics SDKs, or third-party cookies. We do not collect your name, location, or usage patterns for advertising purposes.

How We Use Data

The data stored locally is used solely to provide the core functionality of the app, such as calculating your focus streaks and showing your milestone progress.

Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To alert you when a focus or break session ends.
  • Vibration: To provide tactile feedback when a timer completes.
